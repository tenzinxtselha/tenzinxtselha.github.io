How to choose your college:

-Attend different college fairs. -Do tours of colleges to see if you like the environment. -Focus on the college that has what you would like to study. -Ask people which college they would recommend.

How to stay determined:

-Explore your surroundings -Focus on what you want -Manage your time. -Stay organized -Set goals.

How to choose your major:

-Identify your interests, values, abilities and passions. -Make a list of your strengths and weaknesses. -Explore different majors -Talk to your advisor -Ask questions -Get help.
