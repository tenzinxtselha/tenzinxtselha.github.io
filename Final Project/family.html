How to tell your parents you are growing up:

Show them, take on more responsibilities, it will show them you are maturing. Do what you have to do without having to be told. Actions speak louder than words.

How to tell your parents you are pregnant:

Be honest, have a plan, show them you can handle it and show you are actively planning and preparing.
